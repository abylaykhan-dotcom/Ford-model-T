Ford Model T

Key Facts

  • Introduced: October 1908.
  • Designer: Henry Ford and his engineering team.
  • Production: More than 15 million were built between 1908 and 1927.
  • Engine: 4-cylinder, about 20 horsepower.
  • Top speed: Around 40–45 mph (64–72 km/h).
  • Original price: About $850 in 1908.

    • Body Styles

When Created

The Ford Model T is an automobile that was produced by the Ford Motor Company from October 1, 1908, to May 26, 1927. It is generally regarded as the first mass-affordable automobile.
